Favorites

Steak Fajitas

House Favorite- Fresh Grilled Skirt Steak sliced up with grilled onions, bellpeppers, and garlic butter. Also served with our unique handmade tortillas to give you a flavor you have never had before. Also comes with a setup plate including shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, charro bean soup, mexican rice and pico de gallo.

Brisket Tacos

House Favorite- 3 Grilled Brisket Tacos served with Charro Bean Soup, Mexican rice, and homemade BBQ sauce. Topped with fresh avocado slices, lettuce, onions, and cilantro. One of our most popular dishes.

Papa Nachos

Huge pile of chips with refried beans, melted shredded cheese, and ground beef, along with jalepenos, sour cream, guacamole, pico de gallo, and topped with queso. Sub ground beef with steak or brisket if wanted.

Happy Plate

Simple, but delicious. Mexican rice with fajita beef and queso on top. Choose between fajita chicken, fajita steak, or shrimp.

Brisket Bowl

House Favorite- Rice, lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, and grilled vegetables along with our delicious brisket on top. Served with sour cream and guacamole, and topped with cotija cheese as well. Choose your liking of brisket, fajita chicken, fajita steak, shrimp, or ground beef for our delicious bowls.

Egg Rolls

Try our delicious egg roll appetizer- with cheese, chicken, blackbeans and corn inside. Served with a side of queso.

Grilled Tuna

Delicious and fresh tuna grilled on the mesquite grill, with a side of grilled vegetables on a hot skillet. Also comes with a setup of sour cream, guacamole, rice, cheese, pico, and soup.

Sopapillas

Fried sopapillas with cinnamon, powdered sugar, caramel, chocolate, and whipped cream.

Nutella Crepe

Homemade tortilla filled with nutella and wrapped up like a burrito. Chocolate syrup, powdered sugar, whipped cream, and ice cream along with it.
