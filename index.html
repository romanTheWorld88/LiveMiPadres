House Favorites

Steak Fajitas

Organic and Fresh Grilled Skirt Steak sliced up with grilled onions, bellpeppers, and garlic butter. Also served with our unique handmade tortillas to give you a flavor you have never had before. Also comes with a setup plate including shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, charro bean soup, mexican rice and pico de gallo.

Grilled Red Fish

Delicious grilled red fish served with mixed grilled vegetables of zuchinni squash, broccoli, and grilled onions along our house garlic butter. Also comes with a setup of rice and soup, and our house mango salsa.

Papa Nachos

Huge pile of chips with refried beans, melted shredded cheese, and ground beef, along with jalepenos, sour cream, guacamole, pico de gallo, and topped with queso. Sub ground beef with steak or brisket if wanted.

Happy Plate

Simple, but delicious. Mexican rice with fajita beef and queso on top. Choose between fajita chicken, fajita steak, or shrimp.

Mongolian Beef Tacos

Try our delicious new Mongolian Beef Tacos- three steak tacos with our sweet Mongolian Sauce, topped with grilled green onions, grilled red bell peppers, as well as fresh cabbage and cilantro. Served with Rice and Charro Bean soup on the side, as well as our Spicy Loco Salsa.

Brisket Bowl

House Favorite- Rice, lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, and grilled vegetables along with our delicious brisket on top. Served with sour cream and guacamole, and topped with cotija cheese as well. Choose your liking of brisket, fajita chicken, fajita steak, shrimp, or ground beef for our delicious bowls.

Shrimp Fajitas

Wild Caught and Mesquite Grilled Shrimp- served on a bed of grilled onions, with a fajita setup plate and 3 homemade tortillas. Ask for us to make it spicy for you!

Chicken Bowl

Three grilled chicken enchiladas with our spicy and delicious green swissas sauce on top. Served with rice, beans, sour cream, and pico de gallo.

Red Fish Tacos

Three fresh red fish tacos topped with pico de gallo, cabbage, and sliced avocados. Rice and creamy tortilla soup on the side with our delicious homemade lime salsa.

Steak Tacos Al Carbon

Two large tacos with fajita beef, cilantro, onions, and cheese all cooked together on the grill, then stuffed in our large handmade tortillas. Fan Favorite. Also served with rice, soup, sour cream, guacamole, and pico de gallo.

Lively Atmosphere

Try our delicious egg roll appetizer- with cheese, chicken, blackbeans and corn inside. Served with a side of queso.

Shrimp Quesadilla

Fresh Grilled Shrimp inside large Flour Tortilla with Melted Cheese. Served with Guacamole, Sour Cream, Jalaenos, and Pico on the side.

Steak and Eggs

Delicious Steak Tampicana with two eggs cooked to your liking, as well as Grilled Onions and French Fries with Queso on top. House Favorite!

Nutella Crepe

Homemade tortilla filled with nutella and wrapped up like a burrito. Chocolate syrup, powdered sugar, whipped cream, and ice cream along with it.
